Ontology Tree Visualizer

Intro

This is the ontology tree of the inforMEA thesaurus, where parent-child relation is constructed based on the broader field of the ontology.

Usage

By clicking on a node, its immediate children only will expand/collapse.

By double clicking a node, all the descendent children will expand/collapse as well.

Terms have definition with them, which can be retrieved by hovering the mouse over them.
